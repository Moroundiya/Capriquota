The story behind capriquota

Due to numerous advantages that fully remote teams provide, practically every IT company is now a remote organization. Companies that have made the transition to remote work are now thriving and have a significant recruiting advantage. Instead of recruiting from a small pool of developers near their offices, companies that go remote have access to a global pool of engineers. They are able to not only hire higher-quality employees, but also keep them for longer periods of time. However, establishing a remote-first organization isn't simple.

But becoming a remote-first company isn’t that simple. Companies going for remote teams usually struggle with three things. Firstly, while it is simple to find average developers, it is difficult to find well vetted developers. Secondly, it's difficult to evaluate and vet remote talent in order to find the best candidate. Organizations have a minimal idea about remote developers' fundamental skills and qualifications through a traditional CV. It doesn’t tell them enough about the foreign universities and the companies that a developer went to and worked for, respectively.

Finally, even after finding the right remote development team, many businesses struggle to manage it. In other words, it is hard to have a remote collaboration that's easy, compliant, and secure. So after making research and getting to know the problems top firms encounter getting well vetted talents and also how applicants struggle working remotely the C.E.O of capriquota decided to build something new and capriquota.com came into existence

Capriquota’s approach is a vertically integrated solution that replaces traditional IT service company offerings with an AI-based platform. Capriquota has created the first and only AI-powered tool, a distributed team of developers sourced by software, vetted by software, matched by software, and managed by software.

If you’re a company that needs to scale your engineering capacity quickly, can help you hire from the top 1% of the world’s developers. And if you’re an experienced developer who wants to take your career to the next level, apply to top remote developer jobs at Turing today. With Capriquota’s AI powered tool, the future of work is truly boundaryless.
