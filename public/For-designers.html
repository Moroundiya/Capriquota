How to Get Started

The process required to get your desired job

Register

Show your interest in joining our team by registering at https://capriquota.com/talent please make sure this is the link you're using.

Language and Personality

The first step in the screening process is to conduct a thorough English language and communication interview evaluation with AI powered tools. We also look for people that are enthusiastic about their profession and are completely immersed in it.

In-Depth Skill Review

Every applicant who passes the language and personality tests is put through a series of assessments to evaluate if they have technical knowledge and problem-solving skills. Applicants with exceptional results advance to the next screening phase of our talent pool, which contains experts in a wide range of fields.

Live Screening

Capriquota screens each applicant via individuals who are experts in their functional domain. Our screeners conduct live exercises to assess problem-solving ability, depth of experience, communication ability, and creativity.

Test Projects

Applicants who have progressed this far will be given a test to check if they can “walk the walk”. By participating in complete test projects that simulate real-life events, applicants can demonstrate their competence, thoroughness, professionalism, and integrity.

Personalized Excellence

Get matched to the best cultural and technical fit opportunities.

Why Apply for another Job?
